Oscars Results

He finally won, Leonardo Dicaprio finally won an Oscar. I guess that means no more Dicaprio no Oscar memes. Besides Leonardo Dicaprio winning an oscar for his leading role in The Revenant, there was other news coming from this years oscars. Hosted by Chris Rock, this years oscars was full of surprises and upsets. Best picture this year was won by Spotlight, which was about a Boston criminal court case. Best actress was won by Brie Larson for he role in Room. Supporting actor Mark Rylance won for his role in Bridge of Spies, upseting front runner Sylvester Stallone. Best actress in a supporting role was won by Alicia Vikander for her role in The Danish Girl. Mad Max: Fury Road won the night finishing with six oscars followed by The Revenant's three oscar wins. A movie that has the huge job of rebooting on of the most infulencial movies of all time, has to come out of the gate with a great trailer and this trailer just didn't do it. I feel that this movie should still get a chance which just isn't happening. Looking at the trailer on Youtube it now has more dislikes than likes which is surprising for a movie like this. Even though this trailer didn't really get me excited for this movie there were some things I liked about this. For one, despite the general public opinion, I am not bothered about about an all female team. I feel the gender of the ghostbuster team memembers doesn't really matter for me as long as the movie is good. Another thing that I liked about this trailer is actress, Leslie Jones. I thought by far she was the funniest person in this trailer and was really the only team memeber I really liked coming out of this trailer. The main thing that I didn't like about this trailer the fact they sold this movie as a sequel which I thought is was. The fact that I thought this movie was a sequel for two days until I finally heard it wasn't, is just a terrible job done by the marketing team. Well thats what I thought about the movie and I hope to talk about this more when I review it after it's July 14, 2016 release date.
